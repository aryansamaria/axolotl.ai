How It Works

The Wise Voice Assistant uses Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) technology to provide accurate answers about Wise money transfers.

Voice Input

Your questions are captured using your device's microphone.

Speech-to-Text

Your voice is converted to text using OpenAI's Whisper model.

Knowledge Retrieval

Our system searches for relevant information in its knowledge base.

Answer Generation

A contextually relevant answer is generated.

Text-to-Speech

The answer is converted back to speech and played for you.

Technology Stack

  • Python Flask Backend
  • RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)
  • OpenAI Whisper for Speech Recognition
  • gTTS for Text-to-Speech
  • JavaScript for Interactive Frontend

Data Sources

The information provided by this assistant is sourced from the Wise Help Center and is updated regularly.
